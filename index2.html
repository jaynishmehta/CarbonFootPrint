EMISSIONS ARE CLASSIFIED INTO 3 SCOPES

SCOPE-1 EMISSIONS

Scope-1 emissions refer to direct emissions from sources that are controlled by industry.This includes emissions from on site combustion of fuels in generators,air compressors,other equipments as well as process related emissions

SCOPE-2 EMISSIONS

Scope-2 emissions encompasses indirect emissions from the generation of purchased electricity,heat and steam consumed by industry.These emissions occur at the source of energy generation and not within company control

SCOPE-3 EMISSIONS

Scope-3 emissions cover indirect emission that occur outside of the company operatinal boundary but are associated with its value chain.This includes emissions from purchased goods and services, business travel,employee commuting,transportation and waste disposal
